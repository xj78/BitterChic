about us

explore the difference

At Bitter Chic Clothing, our aim is to design, manufacture and deliver high quality products to our customers at affordable prices. Our product range is focused on fast fashion and includes mens, womens and children clothing.

Our core strengths lies in our highly experienced team. Design, inspection and our network of offshore factories in India, Pakistan and Bangladesh enable us the flexibility to supply products matched to the demands of our customers. We take extra measures to ensure that all our partner factories are compliant to environmental and ethical requirements.

Design

Our approach is very simple!!..

Cost savings

Product range

T-shirts, Polos, Sweatshirts, tops

Knits

With a variety of weights, we can supply knitted dresses, tops, t-shirts, sweatshirts in whole range of colors. We monitor and inspect the entire process chain from yarn, knitting, dyeing/printing, trims and stitching stage until the goods are packed and ready for shipment. A final inspection is carried out before shipment.

Shirts, blouses, skirts, corporate

Wovens

With a variety of patterns, we can supply woven dresses, tops, shirts, skirts, trousers for both; casual and corporte wear in whole range of colors. We monitor and inspect the entire process chain from yarn, weaving, dyeing/printing and stitching stage until the goods are packed, inspected and shiped.

Trousers, shirts, jackets, jeggings, dresses

Denim

As one of our strengths, we can produce denim jackets, trousers(jeans), shirts and patchwork dresses from the highest quality denim producing mills based in Pakistan. With different variety of washes and finishes available, we provide an edge to our customers over their competitors.

Long, skater, one shouldered

Party wear

With a world of fancy fabrics, we can supply an unlimited variety of party dresses suited for all occassions. This process takes a lots of creativity and a sense to utilise different fabrics based on the end use; providing our customers with a chic clothing option.

Hoodies, jogging bottoms, tracksuits

Active Wear

This is a major area of our activities. With our strength in knits, we supply full suits, hooded sweatshirts and jogging bottoms with a mix of blends depending on the end use. Fabrics are treated to add moisture management and quick dry properties.
