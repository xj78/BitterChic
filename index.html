BITTER CHIC CLOTHING

Supplier of high quality fast fashion clothing in the UK at competitive prices. Using a wide variety of fabrics; both knits and woven in 100% cotton, CVC, Denim and Mix of blends of polyester, linen, viscose, acrylic etc.

Fabrics range from Jersey, Pique', French Terry, Fleece, Interlock, Ribs, Thermals etc in Knits and Poplon, Canvas, Plaids, Twill etc in wovens.

Our Demin is very popular in the industry and it includes ring spun, cross hatch, slub and various types of washes.

about us

explore the difference

At Bitter Chic Clothing, our aim is to design, manufacture and deliver high quality clothing to our customers at affordable prices. Our product range is focused on fast fashion and includes womens, mens and children clothing.

Our core strength lies in our highly experienced team. Designing, merchandising inspection alongwith our network of offshore factories in Pakistan, India and Bangladesh enable us the flexibility to supply products matched to the demands of our customers. We take extra measures to ensure that all our partner factories are compliant to environmental and ethical requirements such as ISO, wrap and also vf approved.

our services

Design

Our approach to design is very simple!!..
In addition to supply customer designs, our team of designers work year round on exploring and innovating trends displayed at trade shows. We generate mood boards as per customer brief and offer designs that go in-line with the season.

Cost savings

We are serious about cost & price!!..
We want our customers to compete in the market and offer products best suited to their requirements at an affordable price. We also work with customers in proposing best solutions when cost is of utmost importance.

Reliability

Your 'Trust' means everything to us!!..
Our goal is to be recognised as a reliable supplier to our customers. we have spent countless hours building confidence in our suppliers and are always ready to go the extra mile for our customers. Quality is not to be compromised.

Just In Time

Time is the most valuable thing!!..
We appreciate the importance of time and work through out the production process to maintain critical path and to ensure that the agreed leadtimes are adhrered to and that our customers are always up-to-date with the progress.

Product range

T-shirts, Polos, Sweatshirts, tops

Knits

We supply dresses, tops, t-shirts, sweatshirts in whole range of colors and fabrics such as Jersey, Pique', Fleece, Interlock, French Terry, Thermals etc. We monitor the entire process from yarn, knitting, dyeing/printing, trims and stitching until the goods are packed and ready for inspection/shipment.

Shirts, blouses, skirts, corporate

Wovens

With a variety of patterns, we can supply woven dresses, tops, shirts, skirts, trousers for both; casual and corporate wear in whole range of colors. We monitor and inspect the entire process from yarn, weaving, dyeing/printing and stitching stage. Fabrics such as Poplin, Canvas, Plaids, Twill etc.

Trousers, shirts, jackets, jeggings, dresses

Denim

As one of our strengths, we can produce denim jackets, trousers(jeans), shirts and patchwork dresses from the highest quality denim producing mills based in Pakistan. With different variety of washes/finishes available and denim like ring spun, cross hatch, slub etc, we provide an edge to our customers over their competitors.

Long, skater, one shouldered

Party wear

With a world of fancy fabrics, we can supply an unlimited variety of party dresses suited for all occassions. This process takes a lots of creativity and a sense to utilise different fabrics based on the end use; providing our customers with a chic clothing option for all types of part dresses

Hoodies, jogging bottoms, tracksuits

Active Wear

This is a major area of our activities. With our strength in knits, we supply full suits, hooded sweatshirts and jogging bottoms with a mix of blends depending on the end use. Fabrics are treated to add moisture management and quick dry properties. Ideal for sports brands looking for quality.

Ankle, mid-Calf, Knee High, Thermal

Socks

We can supply basic, mid range and premium socks. Our factories are well renowned and produce all styles and cuts of socks including ankle cut, mid-calf, knee high and thigh high socks. Thermal and also work socks.With a variety of colors and prints to choose from, we offer unbeatable prices.
